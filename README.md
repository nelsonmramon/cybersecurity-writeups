# Cybersecurity Writeups

A record of my hands-on cybersecurity practice — HackTheBox and TryHackMe machines/rooms, documented as I work through them. I'm transitioning into cybersecurity from a software development and U.S. Marine Corps background, and this repo is where I track real, practical progress: what I tried, what worked, and what I learned.

## What's in here
Writeups are organized by platform and tier/difficulty. Each one documents:
- Enumeration steps (what I scanned and found)
- The exploitation path (what actually worked, and why)
- The flag/proof of completion
- The real-world lesson — what this vulnerability class means outside a training lab
